FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about visiting our clinic.

Your first visit includes a comprehensive dental exam, professional cleaning, and a personalized consultation. We'll take X-rays if needed, discuss your goals, and create a tailored treatment plan. The entire experience is designed to be comfortable and informative.

Yes, we accept most major dental insurance plans. Our team will help you maximize your benefits and understand your coverage. We also offer flexible payment plans for treatments not covered by insurance.

We recommend visiting every six months for a routine check-up and professional cleaning. However, depending on your oral health needs, we may suggest more frequent visits. Regular check-ups help prevent problems before they start.

Yes, we provide same-day emergency appointments for urgent dental needs. If you're experiencing severe pain, swelling, or a dental injury, call our office immediately and we'll get you in as soon as possible.

We offer multiple pain management options including local anesthesia, sedation dentistry, and nitrous oxide (laughing gas). Our goal is to ensure your treatment is as comfortable and stress-free as possible.
